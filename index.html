Sleepy Baby - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

At Glanex Digital, your baby’s safety and your privacy are our top priorities. This policy describes how Sleepy Baby handles your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic profile information via Google Sign-in and personalization data—such as the names you provide for your children—to customize and enhance your app experience.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used strictly to provide personalized features, such as greeting your baby by name and maintaining a private sleep diary. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Security

All information is securely stored using Google Firebase infrastructure.

For any questions, please contact us at info@glanexdigital.com
